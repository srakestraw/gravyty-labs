## üè¢ **Logo Usage**

### **Logo Variations**
1. **Primary Logo**: Full color on light backgrounds
2. **White Logo**: White version for dark backgrounds
3. **Horizontal Logo**: Wide format for headers
4. **Stacked Logo**: Vertical format for mobile

### **Logo Guidelines**
- **Minimum Size**: 100px width for horizontal, 50px height for stacked
- **Clear Space**: Equal to the height of the "G" in "Gravyty"
- **Background**: Always maintain sufficient contrast
- **Scaling**: Use vector formats (SVG) for scalability

### **Logo Placement**
- **Headers**: Top-left corner, aligned with navigation
- **Footers**: Centered or left-aligned
- **Mobile**: Stacked version preferred
- **Favicons**: Simplified version for small sizes

## üì± **Digital Guidelines**

### **Web Design**
- **Layout**: Clean, grid-based, responsive
- **Navigation**: Intuitive, consistent placement
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners, clear hierarchy
- **Forms**: Accessible, well-labeled, error states

### **Mobile Design**
- **Touch Targets**: Minimum 44px touch areas
- **Navigation**: Bottom navigation or hamburger menu
- **Typography**: Readable at small sizes
- **Performance**: Optimized images and assets

---

## üé® **UI Components**

### **Buttons**
- **Primary**: Blue background, white text
- **Secondary**: White background, blue text, blue border
- **Ghost**: Transparent background, blue text
- **Destructive**: Red background, white text

### **Cards**
- **Background**: White or light gray
- **Border**: Subtle gray border or shadow
- **Padding**: Consistent spacing
- **Hover**: Subtle elevation or color change

### **Forms**
- **Input Fields**: Clean borders, focus states
- **Labels**: Clear, descriptive text
- **Validation**: Red for errors, green for success
- **Placeholders**: Helpful, not essential information
